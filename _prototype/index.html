FIRST® robotics

Since its inception in 1989, FIRST's mission has been to spark interest in science and technology, primarily amongst young people. It continues to fufill its objective to the current day through a number of programs, including the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC), the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC), and the FIRST Lego League (FLL). For more information, visit their website at www.usfirst.org.
